# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. The dashboard provides valuable insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product categories, payment preferences, and regional sales distribution.

The objective of this project is to transform raw e-commerce transaction data into meaningful visualizations that support business decision-making and performance monitoring.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Sales Analysis by State

Analyzes revenue contribution across different states.

**Insights:**
- Maharashtra generates the highest sales.
- Madhya Pradesh is the second-largest contributor.
- Uttar Pradesh and Delhi contribute relatively lower sales.


### 2️⃣ Quantity Analysis by Category

Displays product demand based on quantity sold.

**Insights:**
- Clothing contributes the largest sales volume.
- Electronics and Furniture follow behind.


### 3️⃣ Monthly Profit Trend

Tracks profit performance month-wise.

**Insights:**
- Strong profitability during January–April.
- Profit declines during May, July, October, and December.
- November records the highest profit.


### 4️⃣ Top Customers Analysis

Identifies customers generating the highest revenue.

**Top Customers:**
- Harivansh
- Madhav
- Madan Mohan
- Shiva


### 5️⃣ Payment Mode Analysis

Visualizes customer payment preferences.

**Payment Methods:**
- UPI
- COD
- Credit Card
- Debit Card
- EMI


### 6️⃣ Profit by Product Sub-Category

Highlights profitability across product segments.

**Most Profitable Sub-Categories:**
- Printers
- Bookcases
- Sarees

## 💡 Business Insights

- Maharashtra contributes the highest revenue.
- Printers are the most profitable sub-category.
- November shows peak profitability.
- Clothing products dominate overall sales quantity.
- A small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue.
- Digital payment methods are widely adopted by customers.
